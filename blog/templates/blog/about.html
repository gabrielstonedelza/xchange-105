About Us

105 XCHANGE RADIO is a US professional and the most prestigious black- owned internet radio. Nestled on the soil of Bronzeville, The web media based firm is established as a global media platform that showcases the true unique identity of the Blacks across the globe. In the light of that, we have come up with an idea of creating a media institution called – (105 XCHANGE RADIO) that reflects the excellence of the black nation. We are not just a media firm, we are also committed to making the world a better place through series of life-changing and positive impactful contribution towards humanity and the entire society. Through our state-of-the-art facility and cutting-edge technologies, we are poised to become the voice for the voiceless. 105 XCHANGE RADIO Station will be a perfect destination of distinguished Africans and individuals held in various positions, class, professions, businesses, and industry without any iota of bias or discrimination. As a herald of peace, we are dedicated to building a united and sustainable Afro community by creating awareness which includes (Africans, African-Americans- Caribbean and Latina). Maintaining friendliness and brotherhood among Africans, we intend to organize an all-inclusive traditional and cultural events in different time frames (quarterly/bi-annual or annually) as well as Africa Festival – Afro Soccer Tournament Campaign against Gun Violence – Afro Boat Cruise).
